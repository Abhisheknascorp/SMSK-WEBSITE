Computer Lab

High-Speed LAN • Windows 10 Systems • Programming & Coding • Digital Safety
Modern Computing Infrastructure

Our school boasts a state-of-the-art computer lab, a hub of technological innovation and learning. Equipped with 40 cutting-edge computers running Windows 10, it serves as a dynamic space where students can explore the digital world and develop essential computer skills.

With a robust LAN connection and high-speed internet access available on each terminal, students can seamlessly connect and collaborate. The lab is further enhanced with a projector and audio-mic system, facilitating interactive learning experiences and presentations.

Conducive Learning Environment

The lab is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration. With a comfortable and ergonomic setup, students can engage in hands-on activities, conduct research, and work on projects independently or in groups. The lab is well-ventilated and equipped with ample power outlets.

Expert Guidance & Curriculum

Dedicated computer teachers provide expert guidance and introduce students to a wide range of software applications, programming languages, and digital tools. From basic computer operations to advanced programming concepts, the curriculum is designed to cater to students of all levels.

The computer lab is not just a place for learning; it's a platform for exploring the vast potential of technology. Students have the opportunity to participate in coding competitions, digital art projects, and online research initiatives. It's a space where young minds are ignited with curiosity and empowered to become digital citizens.

We also educate children about personal safety while using social media to ensure they are fully aware of the health and social risks involved.
