Overview

Our biology laboratory is designed to provide students with a comprehensive and immersive learning experience. The lab boasts an array of excellent features, including:

  • State-of-the-art equipment: Modern microscopes, spectrophotometers, and thermocyclers enable students to conduct experiments with precision and accuracy.
  • Spacious workstations: Ample space for students to work comfortably, promoting collaboration and minimizing distractions.
  • Safety features: Proper ventilation, fire extinguishers, and personal protective equipment ensure a secure environment for students to conduct experiments.
