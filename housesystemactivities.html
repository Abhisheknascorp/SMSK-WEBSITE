Unity • Leadership • Excellence • St. Mary's Spirit
Overview

The House System at St. Mary's High School and Junior College fosters healthy competition and teamwork. It provides a platform where students collaborate across grades to build leadership and community spirit.

Key Objectives

• Leadership: Empowering students through House Captain roles.
• Unity: Building a strong bond between seniors and juniors.
• Growth: Encouraging participation in sports and arts.

The Four Houses
  • Red House: Strength & Courage
  • Blue House: Loyalty & Intelligence
  • Green House: Growth & Harmony
  • Yellow House: Brightness & Creativity
Activity Gallery
Annual Events
  • Athletics: Track, field, and team sports competitions.
  • Arts: Music, dance, and creative drawing contests.
  • Literary: Quizzes, debates, and elocution matches.
Spirit of Excellence

The House System is the heartbeat of St. Mary's student life. It shapes well-rounded individuals ready for future leadership with confidence and integrity.
