Sports & Athletics

Strength • Discipline • Sportsmanship • St. Mary's Glory
Overview

At St. Mary's High School and Junior College, sports are an essential part of the curriculum. We believe that the playing field is where character is built, teaching students the value of hard work, resilience, and teamwork under pressure.

Training Objectives

• Physical Fitness: Developing stamina, agility, and overall health.
• Mental Strength: Building focus and strategic thinking.
• Team Spirit: Learning to win with humility and lose with grace.

Key Disciplines
  • Outdoor: Football, Cricket, Basketball, and Volleyball.
  • Indoor: Table Tennis, Chess, and Badminton.
  • Athletics: Sprints, Relays, Long Jump, and Shot Put.
  • Yoga: Mental well-being and flexibility training.

Annual Sports Meet

Our Annual Sports Day is the highlight of the year, featuring:

  • March Past: A display of house discipline and coordination.
  • Track Events: High-energy 100m, 200m, and 400m races.
  • Trophy Ceremony: Honoring the 'Best Athlete' and the 'Winning House'.
