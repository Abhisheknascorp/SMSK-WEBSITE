Curriculum • Methodology • Evaluation & Assessment • Activities
Curriculum

Curriculum is best defined as all the experiences children have under the guidance of teachers and/or the school. The curriculum of DWPS adheres to the National Curriculum Framework 2005 (NCF 2005) and the CBSE Curriculum.

With experiential learning as guiding principles, DWPS allows each child to realize inner strength and develop originality through self-reflection and lifelong learning.

Early Years Little Angels – Early Years Foundation Stage

Pre-school years are the most vital years as they lay the foundation for future learning and success. Children experience hands-on learning through play, creativity, and exploration.

The well-equipped activity rooms, splash pool, skating arena, language lab, amphitheatre and toy rooms support holistic development and spontaneous learning.

Pre Primary – Thematic Learning

In the KG programme, children are introduced to four main subjects through a theme-based approach. This approach promotes joyful, effective, and lifelong learning.

Methodology

Our methodology is best defined in three words: Play, Learn and Explore. Child-centered programmes focus on cognitive, emotional, social, language and physical development.

Examination Process

The school follows informal assessments for Pre-School and Pre-Primary students using oral, visual and activity-based techniques.

Playful Learning Makes Joyful Learning

Playful learning nurtures curiosity, creativity and emotional development through structured classroom routines and activities.

Coding Lab & Activities

Sorting and categorising shapes helps children develop early mathematical skills and logical thinking.
