School Facilities

STEAM & ROBOTIC LAB

STEAM AND Robotic Lab is an Activity Based Learning (ABL) laboratory comprising of advanced Robots, which can be utilised in experiments by the students in a well-planned and comfortable environment. Each Robot device is accompanied with Robotics, set along with a detailed curriculum which enlists a description of a comprehensive set of concepts for the students.

LIBRARY

The Library is unrivalled with numerous books for student reference and good reading. The library also has Digital content for students of all classes. The school subscribes to sufcient number of newspapers and magazines suitable for students.

COMPUTER LAB

The Computer Lab is equipped with high-speed central servers and the latest educational software (with adequate security and safety features). Every student has access to a terminal in the lab. We also educate children about personal safety while using social media to ensure they are fully aware of the health and social risks involved.

MATH LAB

The Math Lab allows students to explore mathematical concepts via games, puzzles and other modern teaching aids. In addition to teaching in the lab, students are encouraged to use lab facilities on their own to inculcate in them a spirit of discovery and invention.

MEDICAL - INFIRMARY & FIRST AID

The School has a rst aid facility of its own. Regular medical check-ups for the entire school is to be done routinely. Reports of any medical examination will be sent to the parents of students who require special treatment.

TRANSPORTATION FACILITIES

The school has bus facility that covers the town and nearby areas. Specially designed school buses for the safety & comfort of the students are used. The students are picked and dropped by the escorts. The routes are planned in such a way that the travel time of the children is minimised. Parents should consult the School Transport In charge for necessary details.

CAMPUS

The School campus is set to be one of the best campuses in this region. • Embedded with all the amenities for educational excellence. • Located in a lush green area, the campus aims to enthuse and challenge young minds with a stimulating design, environment and international standard facilities. • The premise is equipped with student friendly classrooms, sports & activity areas. • Other facilities such as: - Math Lab. Activity Space. STEAM Lab. Computer Lab Science Lab

E-LIBRARY

In addition to our library, we also have an extensive e-library which can be accessed by students from within the school as well as from home. Students can use the e-library to prepare presentations of their work which also helps them to improve communication skills.

GENERATOR BACK-UP

To ensure that the school always has electricity, so that classes aren’t hampered, and the students may study comfortably without any difculties, the school has a back-up generator facility to ensure that the school has uninterrupted electric supply.

SPORTS

Sports is an integral part of our school’s curriculum. Achievements in the field of sports eclipse all other accomplishments because of the sheer excitement that sporting activities generate.
