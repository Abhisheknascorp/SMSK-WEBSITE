Chairman's Message

St. Mary's High School and
Junior College, Kalina - Mentor

As a Management of St. Mary's High School and Junior College, Kalina, my vision is to promote a 21st century progressive school in Kalina so that migration of students for education from our town at young age stops.

Children of the town bloom and excel in life with great prospects for a bright and prosperous future. To provide quality education, has partnered with Delhi World Foundation, New Delhi and other renowned education service providers of the country thus providing an environment that supports learning, self-development and innovation.

Our focus remains on evolving efficient, confident and highly knowledgeable future citizens with a global perspective, which will further lead and contribute towards the overall development of our great nation. I look forward to a triumphant endeavour as a Chairman of Mary's High School and Junior College, Kalina, and assure you all, that at SMSK, kalina we will have an exciting educational journey which will definitely assist our students in fulfilling all of their dreams.
