Curriculum (NCF 2005)

The Curriculum of Delhi World Public Foundation adheres to the National Curriculum Framework 2005 (NCF 2005). We believe classroom experiences should be organized to permit students to construct knowledge in consonance with their nature and context, providing equal learning opportunities for all.

Our Guiding Principles:

  • Shifting away from rote learning methods.
  • Enriching the curriculum to go beyond textbooks.
  • Teach less to learn more approach.
  • Linking classroom knowledge to life outside the school.
  • Flexible examinations integrated with daily classroom life.
  • Nurturing good citizens with equality and compassion.
