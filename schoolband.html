Overview

At St. Mary's High School and Junior College, the school band is the heartbeat of our campus spirit. We believe that music education fosters teamwork and precision, teaching students the art of synchronization, the mastery of instruments, and the pride of performing for an audience.

Musical Objectives

• Rhythmic Precision: Mastering time signatures and tempo control.
• Ear Training: Developing the ability to harmonize and listen within a group.
• Public Confidence: Performing with poise during parades and ceremonies.

Sections & Instruments
  • Percussion: Side Drums, Bass Drums, and Cymbals.
  • Brass: Trumpets, Bugles, and Trombones.
  • Woodwind: Flutes, Clarinets, and Saxophones.
  • Marching: Drill formations and baton maneuvering.

Ceremonial Events

The band leads all major school functions, including:

  • Investiture Ceremony: Providing the grand fanfare for student leaders.
  • Republic & Independence Day: Leading the ceremonial march past.
  • Inter-School Competitions: Representing the school in regional band meets.
