Welcome to our Lab

"Welcome to the St. Mary's High School and Junior College Chemistry Laboratory, a dynamic and interactive space where curiosity meets discovery by creating bonds with chemicals. Our state-of-the-art lab is designed to provide students with a hands-on learning experience, fostering a deeper understanding of chemistry concepts and principles by observing chemical properties and characteristics.

Equipped with modern apparatus, the latest chemicals, safety equipment, and digital tools, our lab offers an ideal environment for students to explore the wonders of chemistry. From conducting experiments and investigations to analyzing data, making observations and drawing conclusions, our students develop essential scientific skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

Under the guidance of our experienced and passionate teachers, students in our Chemistry lab engage in a wide range of activities, including:

Lab Activities
  1. Practical experiments and investigations
  2. Qualitative and quantitative analysis.
  3. Chemical analysis and synthesis
  4. Separation and purification.
  5. Data collection and interpretation
  6. Scientific research and presentation

By integrating theoretical knowledge with practical applications, our Chemistry lab provides students with a comprehensive learning experience, preparing them for success in their academic pursuits and beyond."
