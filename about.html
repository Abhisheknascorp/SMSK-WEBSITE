About Us

Welcome to St. Mary's High School and Junior College, Kalina

St. Mary's High School and Junior College, Kalina is a pioneering institution dedicated to shaping the future of education. Our focus is on nurturing the all-round development of every student by providing a balanced and holistic learning environment. We aim to build personalities that harmoniously integrate physical, intellectual, emotional, and creative growth.

At St. Mary's, we encourage curiosity, exploration, and a spirit of inquiry in every child. Learning extends beyond classroom walls to include activity workshops, well-equipped laboratories, resource centers, and a rich library. Our approach emphasizes experiential learning, enabling students to learn more by doing, observing, and experimenting, ensuring that education is both meaningful and enjoyable.
