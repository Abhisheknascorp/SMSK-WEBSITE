About Marian Kindergarten Activities

Journey of Lifelong Learning begins…

“Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today”

Curriculum • Methodology • Evaluation & Assessment • Activities

The activities of primary wing are focused towards continuous improvement of the students to explore their latent talents. Splash pool activity, organic farming, STEM robotics, class presentations, special assemblies and celebrations offer opportunities to chisel individual personalities.

Innovative teaching in Discovery Lab, Maths Lab and Computer Lab builds confidence and prepares students for global challenges through experiential learning.

Curriculum

The Primary curriculum provides a broad learning experience and encourages diverse teaching approaches that cater to individual needs. This formative stage enables holistic personal development and skill enhancement.

Methodology

“Children must be taught how to think, not what to think”

The instructional methodology focuses on improving student outcomes through mentoring, technology integration, collaborative learning, experiments, discussions and presentations. A thematic learning approach is adopted for Grade I to build a strong academic foundation.

Evaluation & Assessment

“Your attitude not your aptitude, will determine your altitude”

Student learning is assessed using both formal and informal methods such as group projects, portfolios and class participation. Three cycles of evaluation tests per term assess understanding, reinforce knowledge and build confidence.

The primary wing focuses on moral, cognitive, emotional, imaginative, aesthetic, social and physical development to nurture independent lifelong learners.

Activities

Children are exposed to a wide range of activities such as coding, organic farming, sports, assemblies, inter-house and inter-school competitions, field trips, festival celebrations and international days. These activities reflect a positive school ethos and value-based education.
